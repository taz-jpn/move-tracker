# Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

## Introduction

MoveTracker ("the App") is a mobile application provided by oji-tec ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information in the App.

## Information We Collect

### Location Information
The App uses your device's GPS functionality to provide movement tracking features.

- Data collected: Latitude, longitude, speed, accuracy
- When collected: From when the user starts recording until they stop
- Purpose: Calculate distance traveled, determine transportation mode, calculate scores

### Data Stored Locally
The following data is stored only on your device and is NOT transmitted to any external servers:

- Movement records (date/time, distance, duration, score)
- Collected dot information
- Earned medal information
- App settings (dark mode, screen wake settings, etc.)

## Data Storage Location

All data collected and generated by the App is stored exclusively in your device's local storage. **No data is transmitted to external servers.**

## Sharing with Third Parties

We do not provide, sell, or share any collected information with third parties.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

### OpenStreetMap
We use OpenStreetMap tile data for map display. When retrieving map tiles, your device's IP address may be transmitted to OpenStreetMap servers.

- OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy: https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

## Data Deletion

When you uninstall the App, all data stored on your device will be deleted.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. If there are significant changes, we will notify you within the App.
